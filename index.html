THE FRONTLINERS

India too initiated a host of events to cheer up corona warriors, who are basically frontline workers like doctors, nurses, sanitation workers etc.

During the current crisis, it is the doctors, nurses, policemen, media, sanitation workers, delivery personnel, bank employees, government employees and local store owners who have put their lives on the line to ensure that we as a nation are protected and cared for adequately

it’s time to bring their stories of bravery and determination to a bigger audience, so they may become symbols of our collective spirit during a tough time for the country.
